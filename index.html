Biography

My name is Tianyi Hao (郝天一). Welcome to my homepage!

I am currently a Ph.D. student on computer science in the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS) at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Longbo Huang. I was working as an intern in Big Data Lab, Baidu Research from 2015 to 2017.

Before that, I have finished my undergraduate study in Yao class at Tsinghua, and received my B.E. degree in July 2014.
