Biography

My name is Tianyi Hao (郝天一). Welcome to my homepage!

I am currently a third year Ph.D. student on computer science in the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS) at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Longbo Huang. I have been working as an intern in Baidu Big Data Lab (BDL) since June 2015.
